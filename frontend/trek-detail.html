Trek Overview

Experience the rugged beauty of the Himalayas on this premier trek. Our Singla Pass route offers unparalleled views of the Ganesh Himal and Manaslu ranges, taking you through remote Tamang villages and dense rhododendron forests.

Security Protocols

warning

Altitude Awareness

Reaching 4,050m requires caution. Our guides are trained in recognizing Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS) symptoms early. We carry pulse oximeters and medical kits.

medical_services

Emergency Evacuation

In case of severe injury or illness, we maintain 24/7 communication. Helicopter evacuation protocols are pre-established with providers.
